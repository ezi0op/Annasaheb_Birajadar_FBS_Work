Life of Cricket Player

As a person, MS Dhoni is humble, disciplined, and down-to-earth. He believes in hard work and staying calm under pressure. Despite achieving great success, he lives a simple life and respects everyone around him.

Army Person

MS Dhoni is an honorary Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Territorial Army. He has completed training with the Parachute Regiment and deeply respects the Indian Armed Forces. His discipline and dedication reflect his love for the nation.

International Player

MS Dhoni represented India in all formats of international cricket. Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. He is one of the most successful captains in cricket history.
